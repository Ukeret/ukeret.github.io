About Us

Uk and Associates Co, a strategic partner with Catch Fly Factory, is a premium supplier of handcrafted fishing flies and associated materials. Based in Philadelphia, PA, USA, we specialize in creating high-quality, innovative fishing flies that cater to both novice anglers and seasoned fly fishing enthusiasts. We also serve commercial and retail businesses, offering customized solutions for their unique needs.

With a deep respect for the environment and the traditions of fly fishing, we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional products that enhance their fishing experiences. Our passion for fly fishing drives us to offer high-quality flies tailored to your specifications.
